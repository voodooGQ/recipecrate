# Tzatziki Sauce

## Ingredients

- 4g garlic
- 1 lemon's juice
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 100g cucumber
- 1 tsp dill weed
- 215g Greek yogurt
- 70ml milk
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Seed and peel the cucumber.
2. Mince cucumber and garlic.
3. Juice lemon.
4. Mix all together
5. If using regular plain yogurt omit the milk

---

- Prep: 10m
- Total: 10m

Yield: About 1 cup
